Products — FINO Suite

FINO Suite uses artificial intelligence for intelligent data collection and validation. Users can communicate through natural dialogue and receive expert guidance based on a connected knowledge database.

  • FINO ChatBot — Interactive AI assistant for your website

    An AI assistant that integrates easily into websites. It helps users quickly find the right information, answers frequently asked questions, and explains administrative procedures. Always up-to-date knowledge, multilingual support, accessible communication, GDPR compliant. Available 24/7.

  • FINO FormBot — Intelligent assistant for your forms

    An AI assistant that guides applicants step by step through complex forms. Through natural conversation, it captures all required information and automatically transfers it into the form. Multilingual support, accessible communication, data validation, GDPR compliant. Up to 80% faster form completion.

  • FINO Upload-Checker — AI‑powered document analysis

    An AI solution for automatic verification of uploaded documents. The system recognizes document types, extracts relevant data, and validates their authenticity. Multi-format support, OCR technology, GDPR compliant. Reduces manual verification costs by up to 90%.

  • FINO Knowledge Hub — Custom knowledge bases

    A central platform for creating, managing, and maintaining knowledge bases. Upload documents and sources, have them automatically indexed, and manage tenant-specific knowledge areas with versioning. Semantic search, MCP protocol, GDPR compliant.

  • FINO Connect — Making knowledge accessible (MCP Server, MS Teams, REST API)

    A universal access interface for knowledge bases. Ensures knowledge is available everywhere — via MCP Protocol, REST API, MS Teams, and other channels. Read-only access, multi-channel delivery, scalable from individual integrations to enterprise-wide deployments.

Detailed information about FINO Suite AI solutions, including the option to try demo versions, can be found on the website of our partner bol Behörden Online Systemhaus GmbH.
